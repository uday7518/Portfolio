Description

During the Vignan Srujankura technical fest, our team participated in a 24-hour hackathon where we developed an Android application called Subscription Management System. This application simplifies the process of managing subscriptions for popular OTT (Over-The-Top) platforms. Overall, our Subscription Management System offers users a convenient way to keep track of their OTT subscriptions and ensures that they never miss out on their favorite content due to expired plans. The use of Firebase as the backend infrastructure enhances the reliability and performance of our application.
