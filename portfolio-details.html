Description

Our team successfully tackled a real-time problem faced by our university by developing an Android application as part of our IDP. The primary objective was to streamline the semester registration process, eliminating the need for paper-based submissions. Overall, our Android application for semester registration has streamlined the registration process, making it more efficient, transparent, and environmentally friendly by eliminating paper-based submissions. The use of Google Firebase backend and Google API for messaging ensures reliability and seamless communication throughout the registration process.
